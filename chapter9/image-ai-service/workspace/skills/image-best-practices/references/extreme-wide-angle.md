# Extreme Wide Angle Photo Edit

**Use when**: ユーザーが極端な広角レンズ効果（超広角/魚眼）の
写真編集、またはスマートフォン画面の内容差し替えを要求した時。
既存の人物写真の EDIT 前提。

## Template

Edit the provided photo with an extreme wide-angle lens effect
(equivalent to 12-18mm fisheye).
Change camera angle to [ANGLE: looking up/looking down/extreme
low/high/tilted].
Bring [BODY PART: hands/feet/face/knees] extremely close to the
camera with exaggerated perspective.
Subject holds smartphone with [GRIP: one-handed/two-handed/
casual/close to chest].
Replace only the smartphone screen with [SCREEN CONTENT].
Preserve: person's face, hairstyle, clothing, and environment
type, lighting direction and intensity.

## Example

"Edit photo with extreme wide-angle fisheye effect (12mm
equivalent), low angle looking up. Bring subject's feet and lower
legs extremely close to camera. Subject holds phone casually at
waist height, one-handed grip. Replace phone screen with the city
map image. Preserve face, outfit, and outdoor street environment."
