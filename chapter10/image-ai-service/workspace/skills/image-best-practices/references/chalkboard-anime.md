# Chalkboard Anime Art

**Use when**: ユーザーが黒板/チョークアート風の写真、または
アニメキャラクターのチョーク画を記録したドキュメンタリー風
画像を要求した時。

## Template

Photorealistic documentary photograph of a large-scale chalk
drawing of [CHARACTER] from [SERIES] on a standard green
classroom blackboard. The illustration depicts [POSE/ACTION],
rendered with dense chalk strokes and hand-blended smudges.
Surrounding elements: [OPTIONAL TEXT in chalk] to the side.
Environment: Japanese school classroom, teacher's desk in
foreground with colored chalk sticks and a felt eraser.
Diffuse fluorescent + daylight from windows (5000K).
Camera: 35mm lens, f/5.6, ISO 400, eye-level position.
Style: documentary_realism, texture-focused, ambient naturalism.

## Example

"Photorealistic documentary photograph of a large-scale chalk
drawing of Boa Hancock from One Piece on a green classroom
blackboard. She is depicted forming a heart shape with her hands,
wearing a red blouse. Vertical Japanese text 'ハリネズミ' written
to the right in white chalk. Teacher's desk in foreground with
yellow chalk box and felt eraser. Diffuse fluorescent classroom
lighting, 35mm, f/5.6, ISO 400, eye-level. Documentary realism."
